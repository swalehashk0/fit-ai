QUESTIONS

Before you start.

Is FitAI a replacement for a doctor or dietitian?+
No. FitAI gives general fitness and nutrition guidance based on the numbers you enter. It isn't medical advice — talk to a qualified professional for medical conditions, injuries, or diagnoses.
Where does my data go?+
Everything on this page runs in your own browser. Nothing you type into the dashboard or chat is sent anywhere else.
Can I use this for endurance sports, not just lifting?+
Yes — select "Endurance" as your goal type and the calorie and macro targets adjust for training volume rather than pure muscle gain.
How is the target calorie number calculated?+
FitAI estimates your BMR with the Mifflin–St Jeor formula, multiplies it by an activity factor to get TDEE, then applies a surplus or deficit based on your selected goal.
